About Me

Designer.
Creator.
Storyteller.

I'm Subhasree Sengupta, a graphic designer based in Bangalore, India. I specialise in brand identity, print design, motion, and digital visual communication — creating work that communicates feeling, not just information.

My process starts with understanding the story a brand needs to tell, then finding the visual language to tell it — from the first logo sketch to the final billboard or motion poster.
