Candelia is a Bangalore-based handcrafted candle brand built around the idea that a single flame can shift the energy of an entire space. The brief called for a complete visual identity — from logo and colour palette, through printed stationery and packaging, to outdoor advertising and social assets.

Every decision was rooted in three core values: warmth, hope, and quiet luxury. The result is a cohesive system that feels tactile and artisan at every touchpoint.

01 / Logo Identity

The Logo Story

The wordmark merges the letter C with a candle flame — a mark that is immediately legible and deeply symbolic. The C cradling the flame as warmth cradling life, hope, and positivity.

Logo Breakdown & Brand StoryConstruction · Colour variants · Typography · Brand values

"The name Candelia is inspired by the word 'candle' — combined with a soft, elegant ending that represents light, warmth, and grace."
